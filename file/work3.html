OVERVIEW

In the ever-evolving landscape of travel and hospitality, XTREME sought to highlight the unique benefits of their properties through their “XTREME VIDEO” campaign. This spot was XTREME’s addition the series of spots.

PRE-PRODUCTION

CRAFTING THE CONCEPT

The moments we remember most on vacation always seem to be the little ones — the unplanned ones, the quiet ones. We wanted to develop a script that highlighted just this: the magic of simple vacation moments that make trips special.

PRODUCTION

BRINGING THE VISION TO LIFE

In one day in a gorgeous rental on XTREME, we shot our actors loving and living it up, playing out an ideal Extreme trip.

POST-PRODUCTION

FINE-TUNING THE WORK

In the edit, we realized so much of what we captured was unplanned — and that made for a perfect portrayal of the easy-going nature of a relaxing vacation.
